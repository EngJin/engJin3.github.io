Frequently Asked Question

Can't find the answer you're looking for? We've shared some of out most frequently asked question to help you!

How much is the delivery fee?

Items purchased with a value up to RM50 will be automatically free of shipping. Less than RM50 will be charged a shipping charge of RM8.

The delivery area

The delivery service of our store is limited to the west-Malaysia area only

Refund

We only refund the full amount paid due to out of stock. No other reasons are acceptable.

Delivery Rejected

If the delivery rejected, the buyer needs to go to the warehouse to pick up the item in person. If for any reason you cannot pick up the item in person, we will not reimburse you. If you want a second delivery, you need to pay for one more delivery.

Parcel damaged

If any damage is found, you need to take photos and contact the customer service within three days. After verification, the customer service will make compensation.No compensation will be made after three days.
